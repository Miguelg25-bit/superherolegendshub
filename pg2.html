Superman

Superman, also known as Clark Kent, is an alien from the planet Krypton. He has superhuman strength, flight, heat vision, x-ray vision, and near invulnerability. His story revolves around protecting Earth as its greatest hero.

Batman

Batman, aka Bruce Wayne, is a genius detective and master martial artist. He relies on his intelligence, technology, and physical prowess to fight crime in Gotham City. Unlike most heroes, Batman has no superpowers.

Wonder Woman

Wonder Woman is an Amazonian princess with super strength, agility, and combat skills. She wields the Lasso of Truth and indestructible bracelets. Her mission is to bring peace and justice to the world.
