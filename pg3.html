Spider-Man

Spider-Man, aka Peter Parker, gained superpowers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He has enhanced agility, strength, the ability to stick to walls, and a precognitive "spider-sense." Known for his witty humor and sense of responsibility, Spider-Man fights to protect New York City while balancing his life as a high school student and a superhero.

Iron Man

Iron Man, or Tony Stark, is a billionaire inventor and industrialist who created an advanced suit of powered armor. With technology-enhanced flight, repulsor blasts, and artificial intelligence, Iron Man uses his genius and resources to battle powerful enemies and lead the Avengers. He represents the peak of human innovation and courage.

Captain America

Captain America, Steve Rogers, was transformed into a super-soldier through a secret government experiment during World War II. He is known for his unmatched combat skills, tactical leadership, and his indestructible vibranium shield. As a symbol of hope and justice, Captain America fights for liberty and inspires generations of heroes.
