Thor

Thor is the Norse God of Thunder, wielding the enchanted hammer Mjolnir. He commands lightning, can fly, and possesses godlike strength and durability. As a founding member of the Avengers, Thor fights with honor to protect both Asgard and Earth.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, aka Black Widow, is a master spy and elite combatant. Trained in the Red Room, she brings stealth, strategy, and deadly precision to the Avengers. Despite lacking superpowers, her courage and skills make her a formidable hero.

Hulk

The Hulk, Bruce Banner, transforms into a powerful green giant when enraged. His strength is virtually limitless, growing with his fury. While he struggles with control, Hulk is a mighty force for good and a key part of the Avengers team.
