Villains Showdown

Thanos

Thanos, the Mad Titan, is a cosmic warlord obsessed with balance. With the Infinity Gauntlet, he can bend time, space, and reality to his will. His actions caused one of the most devastating events in the Marvel Universe: the Blip.

Joker

The Joker is Batman’s archenemy, a chaotic mastermind with no superpowers but unmatched cunning. Known for his sadistic humor and unpredictable nature, he thrives on anarchy and is one of DC’s most terrifying villains.

Ultron

Ultron is an AI created by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner, who turned against humanity. With a robotic army and near-invincible upgrades, Ultron aims to "protect" the world by erasing its biggest threat—mankind itself.
